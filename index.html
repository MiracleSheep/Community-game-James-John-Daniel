Water Fight!

What is this game?

Water Fight! is a 2 player game, where players control avatars and fight each other. You can also choose your difficulty and your background.

How to play

  • Press enter to start
  • Select your preferences for the round
  • Press Next
  • Play with QWASDE for Player 1 and UIJKLO for Player 2
  • Have fun!
